About

Thom "Spebby" Mott is a California based university student and part-time developer focused on interactive digital media specialising in Game AI and Computer Graphics. As an undergrad, Thom was affiliated with the SET lab and SLOTHLab, where he contributed to the development of "Compassionate AR", and "Procedural Content Generation in Minecraft". This, and his personal work has lead him to draw on a breadth of creative influences, taking from both technical and human-centred approaches.

Outside of academics, Thom has been working on and around games for over a decade, getting started with video game modding in the 2010's, releasing multiple officially endorsed 'curated' maps for the game Unturned, where he got his start in game and level design. At the same time, he has published several games in collaboration with fellow modders on Newgrounds, and is currently working on the upcoming action-roguelike Gamba Gun.
